# Synthetic Payment Incident Runbook

Trigger: payment queue latency above the internal threshold for ten minutes.

The on-call engineer opens the local incident template and collects a payment incident evidence pack:
timeline, customer impact summary, rollback decision, command transcript, and links to cited runbook
sections. The evidence pack remains under the local Ragmir reports folder until the incident lead
approves external sharing.

Do not paste raw customer records into an agent prompt. Use cited retrieval snippets and summarize
uncertainty separately from confirmed evidence.
